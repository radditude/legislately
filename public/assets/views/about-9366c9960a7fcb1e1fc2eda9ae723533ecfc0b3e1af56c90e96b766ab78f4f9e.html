About

LegisLately is hand-crafted and maintained by the one and only CJ Horton using AngularJS, Ruby on Rails, Bootstrap, and Devise.

Data is provided by the ProPublica Congress API, with address searching through the Google Civic Information API.

Feedback and contributions are welcomed on Github. Criticisms should be carefully written down, rolled into a ball, and tossed gently into the nearest body of water (or you could open a Github issue. Whatever).
